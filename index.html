The Process

About Kayla

Microbiology. Dental school. Cancer.
Then real estate.

It's not the typical path to becoming a broker but it's mine, and it shaped everything about how I do this work.

The Origin Story

Why I do this the real version

"Becoming a top agent was never my dream. But helping people through one of the biggest decisions of their life? That part I fell into and I never looked back."

I have a degree in Microbiology with a minor in Chemistry, and I was about a year into dental school well on my way to becoming an orthodontist when I was diagnosed with a rare cancer called Ewing's Sarcoma. I came back to Nashville for treatment. And during that time, my then-fiancé and I bought our first home. That's when I fell in love with the real estate transaction.

What I discovered was that the same critical thinking and problem-solving that made me good in a lab made me great in a negotiation. I see real estate transactions the same way I once saw complex experiments every variable matters, every detail has a consequence, and preparation is everything.

Over 9 years and $100M+ in Middle Tennessee transactions, I've built genuine relationships with lenders, inspectors, attorneys, title companies, and agents across this market. I know who does excellent work and who doesn't. And I've never stopped believing that the most valuable thing I can offer any client is the same thing: honesty.

BuyUnrepped grew out of that conviction that buyers deserve real support even when they choose not to use a traditional buyer's agent. And my matchmaking work for sellers grew out of the same thing: the belief that connecting people with exactly the right partner is more valuable than doing everything yourself.
